# Internationalization Standard

## Purpose

Maintain complete Hebrew RTL and English LTR behavior.

## Mandatory rules

Translate every user-facing string, set lang/dir semantically, use logical CSS, and format locale-sensitive values.

## Recommended practices

Use typed keys, interpolation, content-language labels, and direction-neutral component structure.

## Forbidden practices

Hard-coded UI copy, concatenated grammar, left/right layout assumptions, or mirroring semantic/non-directional icons.

## Example

Translate a whole sentence with variables rather than joining fragments.

## Review checklist

Review key parity, overflow, order, dates/numbers, accessible names, and both directions.

## Related validation

`unit key checks, RTL/LTR Playwright, axe, visuals`

## Exceptions process

Document the need, risk, smallest scope, owner, expiry or removal plan, and compensating tests. Material or durable exceptions require an accepted ADR and security/QA review.
