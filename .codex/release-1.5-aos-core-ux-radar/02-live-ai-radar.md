# Version 1.5 Live AI Radar

## Contract

Radar accepts external payloads only as `unknown`, validates a bounded versioned schema and approved HTTPS hosts, deduplicates canonical stories, and retains seven calendar days. Favorites persist locally and preserve older saved records. Cached, online, offline, unavailable, and partial states are explicit.

## Data lifecycle

The release includes a human-reviewed cache and a manual same-origin refresh. History, favorites, payload size, record count, strings, and diagnostics have explicit bounds. Automated research creates immutable artifacts and never publishes or commits research output.

## Editorial boundary

Only accountable primary sources enter the reviewed feed. Every title, summary, translation, implication, checksum, and source date requires human review through a normal pull request.
